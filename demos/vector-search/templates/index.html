📡 MISSION BRIEF — OPERATION: SEMANTIC COMPASS
Deep in the ScyllaDB cluster, NEBULA-7 has vectorized every product in the catalog. Each item carries a 16-dimensional embedding — a mathematical fingerprint of its meaning. When you search, your query is projected into the same space and ScyllaDB's vector-store sidecar returns the nearest neighbors using cosine similarity. No keywords. No filters. Pure semantic distance. The Coodie Corps built the index with a single annotation: VectorIndex(similarity_function="COSINE").
